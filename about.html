About SLN Cloud Consulting

Driving digital transformation through innovation and expertise

Our Vision: Business Transformation with Ease

SLN Cloud Consulting is the fastest growing IT Services Company and has evolved into a trusted solution provider for many customers from various industries.

We specialize in healthcare, energy, insurance, high-tech, internet services and utilities, and public sectors. Our services span from ERP implementations, Managed Services, Product Development and Staff Augmentation.

Our team brings several years of combined industry experience with the aforementioned technologies and sets the standard for excellence with successful, timely, and cost-effective implementations.

Founded by a group of IT Professionals with decades of experience across the globe, many of our team members were Oracle product leaders. As a trusted partner for business, we believe that open communication with our clients is the key to developing relevant solutions.
